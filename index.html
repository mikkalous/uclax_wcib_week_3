Have you heard of viviparous tomatoes before?
It's when a seedling starts growing while it's still inside a full grown tomatoe.
It looks a little gross, but once you get used to it, it's an amazing thing!


The below is not really a recipe nor directions. It's more of what happened.

How to Grow Viviparous Tomatoes During COVID-19

Ingredients & Materials

  • 2 to 3 full matured tomatoes (preferably on the vine)
  • 1 cool, dark space to store
  • 1 bright, warm space to store
  • 1 very strong stomach that's not squeamish
  • a lot of time preferably lot of time indoors, with nothing to do
  • 1-10 cotton mask or face covering, preferably reusable
  • 100 ml of hand sanitizer
  • 1 functioning credit card
  • 1 reliable mode of transportation, car, leg & foot

Instructions

  1. Wash hands for at least 20 seconds with warm water & soap
  2. Put on face covering or mask
  3. Squeeze 20 ml of hand sanitizer into portable bottle
  4. Put portable bottle in pocket along with credit card
  5. Use preferable mode of transportation to nearest grocery store
  6. Purchase 2-3 on-the-vine tomatoes
  7. Pay with credit card to reduce chances of human contact & contamination
  8. Use preferable mode of transportation to quarantined headquarters
  9. Place grocery bag on floor along with contaminated items
  10. Wash hands again for 20 seconds with warm water & soap
  11. Remove face covering
  12. Sanitize all contaminated items, such as phone, keys, credit card
  13. Pray the store-bought tomatoes do not have COVID-19 on them
  14. Wash hands again (20 secs minimum!)
  15. Take tomatoes out of bag & place in dark spot
  16. Forgot about the tomatoes for about 1 to 2 months
  17. Find alien looking squiggly sprouts growing out from the tomatoes
  18. Be grossed out by said squiggly wormy looking sprouts
  19. Get over it & place the now viviparous tomatoes in the sunny spot
  20. Rotate placing the viviparous tomatoes between the sunny & dark spaces
  21. Grow sprouts into full fruit producing tomatoes
  22. You are now a tomatoe farmer
  23. You now live off the grid
  24. Grow beard
  25. Live off land
  26. Change your name to Marven
  27. This is your life now
